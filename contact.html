Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to recover a lost item?

The time it takes to recover a lost item varies depending on several factors such as how quickly it's reported, the item's distinctiveness, and whether someone has found it. On average, items are recovered within 3-7 days when reported promptly.

Is there a fee for using your service?

No, our basic service is completely free. We believe in helping people reconnect with their lost items without financial barriers. However, we may introduce premium features in the future that could have associated costs.

What should I do if I find an item?

If you find an item, please report it through our "Report Found" form with as much detail as possible. Include photos if you can. This helps the rightful owner identify their property. We'll guide you through the process of safely returning the item.

How can I verify that an item belongs to me?

When claiming an item, we recommend providing specific details that only the owner would know, such as unique markings, contents, or serial numbers. For valuable items, we may facilitate a secure meeting with our team present to verify ownership.

What areas in Cameroon do you cover?

We currently operate in major cities across Cameroon including Bamenda, Douala, Yaoundé, Buea, and Limbe. Our services are expanding to cover more regions. Even if you're outside these areas, you can still use our platform to report lost or found items.
